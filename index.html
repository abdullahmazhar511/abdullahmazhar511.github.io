I am a Ph.D. Candidate at IIIT Delhi, working under the guidance of Dr. Md. Shad Akhtar at the FLaMe Lab. I completed my undergraduate degree in Computer Science and Engineering at the Institute of Engineering and Rural Technology (IERT), Allahabad, India. My research interests lie at the intersection of Natural Language Processing (NLP), Multimodal Systems, and Responsible AI for Mental Health. My research journey spans over two years, with publications including an Oral paper at The Web Conference (WWW) 2025 and in the Main Track of NeurIPS 2025. Previously, I worked as a Project Officer at IIT Madras, where I contributed to the Bhashini initiative by building end-to-end Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) and Text-to-Speech (TTS) pipelines for Indian languages. Before that, I worked as a Data Scientist at Birlasoft and a Software Developer at Analytics2AI.

To know more, refer to my resume or drop me an email!
